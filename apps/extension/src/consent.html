Before you use group posting

This is higher-risk and you use it at your own risk. Automating activity on Facebook — even assisted — can lead Facebook to restrict, suspend, or permanently ban your account. Only you can decide if that risk is acceptable.

How Presence protects you (these limits cannot be turned off):

  • Randomized, jittered delays between posts — never a fixed interval.
  • A daily cap, with a slow warm-up ramp for new/quiet accounts.
  • Posting only within the active hours you set.
  • Blocks near-identical copy-paste content across groups.
  • Assisted & confirmed: you review and click Post — nothing is posted silently.

What Presence will never do:

  • Run this on our servers, or post on your behalf without you.
  • Ask for or store your Facebook password.
  • Use proxies, fingerprint spoofing, or any ban-evasion tricks.
